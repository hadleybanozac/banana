ABOUT THIS HERE BANANA

Hadley Banozac is a Chicago based artist specialing in artful nonsense. She self identifies as Queen of the Banana Kingdom, a place that by reading this you have now become a citizen of. Other titles include Potato Farmer, Pumpkin Princess, Purple Child and Frog Leader.

Hadely works with video and new media (but NOT! film!) exploring subjects ranging from navigating femininity to control over image and identity online to internet humor and aesthetics.
